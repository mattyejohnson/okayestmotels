This motel earned five crashed cars because it had cool lights, was clean, and we encountered no issues while we stayed there.

Address: 40 S 193rd East Ave Catoosa, OK 74108

Stray Observations
  • There are blue lights attached to the outside of the motel which look super cool at night.
  • The person at the front desk was very nice. We arrived past around two am, and they didn't seem to mind.
  • The room was very clean, and contained enough furniture to suitably barricade the door if one wished to.
  • If you ask people who live in Tulsa and surrounding areas, they will tell you that Catoosa is one of the sketchier places (with a decent amount of robberies and such) in the area. Whether this is an issue or not is up to you, but it's worth noting when choosing a place in or near Tulsa to stay.
