This motel earned five crashed cars because it was clean, the front desk people were nice, the room was comfortable and had a solid lock, and we didn't encounter any issues.

Address: 12525 East 52nd St South, Tulsa, 74146, OK

Stray Observations
  • The front desk had a bunch of cool decorations. I believe some of them are related to spirituality or religion.
  • The people at the front desk were very nice.
  • The whole place was very clean.
  • A lot of the parking lot is on a steep incline, with signs pointing it out. As long as you have a decent parking break, you should be fine.
  • There is a nearby Super 8 that your navigation service might take you to. This is not the correct motel, and the font desk people will probably look long-suffering about you arriving at the wrong place. Apparently it happens to them a lot.
  • The room door locked very securely.
