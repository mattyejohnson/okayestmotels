This motel earned five crashed cars because the theme was cool and we had no problems. The two things worth noting were uncomfortable beds and the front desk scanning our IDs upon arrival. These are two things that might deter some travelers, but didn't bring down our rating of the motel.

Address: 100 West F street Casper, WY 82601
Phone: 844-709-9779

Stray Observations
  • This motel is boat-themed, which is cool. The first-floor rooms have concrete walls around the doors, which does evoke the feeling of wandering around the cargo hold of a boat.
  • The front desk requires copies of everyone's ID if they are over 18.
  • There are quiet hours. If I remember correctly, they started at 10pm. This could be an issue if you want to talk loudly/watch TV past then.
  • The microwave was pretty loud, but it worked just fine.
  • The beds weren't horribly comfortable.
