About Us

Who doesn't love not paying a ton of money for a motel, especially when you're roadtripping or traveling on a budget? Here at Okayest Motels, we review cheap motels that we have personally stayed at. Everyone has comfort different levels re: discomforts or sketchiness they're willing to deal with, so we do our best to include all of the relevant information about any particular motel so that you can decide what you're okay dealing with. If a room had bedbugs, we'll tell you. If we were threatened or harassed in the area, we'll tell you. If the room wasn't clean, we'll note how. We only include motels that cost less than $80 a night for same-day booking (for their cheapest room, generally one king bed).

Our Rating System:
Here are the negative things that a room rated with each number of broken down cars could have had:
The motel caught on fire with us inside / Someone mugged us or a gunshot wound was obtained / There were bedbugs
The room was incredibly dirty / There were hidden fees upon arrival / The door didn't lock
The staff was incredibly rude without being provoked / Something major (like the fridge or shower) didn't work / Had to pay for parking
There were some elements of the room that could have been cleaner / Something minor (like a hair dryer) didn't work / The staff was moderately rude without being provoked
None of the above applied / The stay was generally issue-free and there was nothing negative to note

All information on this website is only accurate as of the reviewer's stay. No information is guaranteed to be current and accurate. All information is based on the reviewer's own experience.
